What is the Temperature Map?

This map shows the distribution of heat on the Earth's surface and the atmosphere in real-time or projected. Temperature is influenced by solar radiation, altitude, latitude, proximity to the sea, and atmospheric circulation patterns, making it fundamental to the climate.

Positive Aspects

  • • Suitable climate for agriculture and ecosystem growth.
  • • Allows for human habitability in various areas of the planet.
  • • Influences the water cycle, which is essential for life.
  • • Facilitates the prediction of heat or cold waves for preparation.

Negative Aspects

  • • Global warming causes extreme weather events.
  • • Heat waves leading to dehydration and risk of death.
  • • Sub-zero temperatures that damage crops and infrastructure.
