Wind Map

What is the Wind Map?

This map shows the air circulation patterns around the planet in real-time. Winds are formed by differences in temperature and atmospheric pressure, and follow predictable patterns influenced by the Earth's rotation.

Positive Aspects

  • • Generate clean and renewable wind energy.
  • • Help regulate global temperatures.
  • • Facilitate the dispersal of seeds and pollen.
  • • Power maritime navigation.

Negative Aspects

  • • Can form destructive storms and hurricanes.
  • • Cause soil erosion in arid areas.
  • • Complicate commercial flights.
  • • Contribute to the spread of wildfires.
